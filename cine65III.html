CiNE65 IV: Hello Pa

A short film that I directed and co-wrote with the team behind the project. The film was submitted to the fourth edition of CiNE65. It was awarded the best screenplay award.

I served as the director of the film and co-wrote the script with the five-person team
that worked on this project.

The film went on to win the Best Screenplay award at CiNE65 IV. It was played during the ad time before movies at Golden Village cinemas in Singapore.
