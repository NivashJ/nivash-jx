The Straits Times

Photojournalism
Intern and Freelance
Singapore Press Holdings

Fueled by a passion for journalism and photography, I embarked on a journey into the captivating realm of photojournalism, seamlessly uniting my two passions. I was fortunate enough to be presented with the opportunity to pick up an internship at the Straits Times during my third year at Ngee Ann Polytechnic. After my stint as an intern, I signed on as a stringer for the paper, occasionally contributing where needed.
