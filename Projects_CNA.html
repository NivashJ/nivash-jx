Channel News Asia: Apassionato

Documentary produced for Channel News Asia's "On the Red Dot"
Season 1: Episode 37 | Aired December 2016

Apassionato is a short documentary that was produced in collaboration with Channel News Asia (Mediacorp). It was featured as part of their current affairs/info-ed program "On the Red Dot". The ideation and execution of various aspects such as production, liason, and post-production was handled by a 5-person student team; I primarily served as the director of photography for this project.

The episode which premiered on 23 December 2016 as part of Episode 37 Season 1 can be watched on MeWatch. It can also be found in the National Archives of Singapore.
