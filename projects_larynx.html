Human Larynx 3D model: Physiological phonetics project

Modelled the human larynx in Blender for a linguistics physiological phonetics project.

The end goal was to develop an anatomical model of a speech or hearing related structure. While creating an analog model moulded from plasticine would have sufficed, I challenged myself to craft a digital 3D model using Blender, an open source 3D modelling software. Prior to the project, I had dabbled with 3D modelling out of curioisty and interest in my own time; as such, I was excited to finally put my skills to the test by attempting my first real project.

Towards the end, while exploring different ways to present the model, I decided to push my ambition just a little further by implementing Augmented Reality to my project. Eventually, after some research and trial, I managed to make it such that my model could be viewed in AR.
